Cooking PAPA

The days of stressing over cooking steps, gathering ingredients or estimating serving portions wrongly are now be behind you. Cooking PaPa is the latest solution that brings all these features and more. Based on your mood, inventory and skill, Cooking PaPa will dive deep to gather recipes that meet your needs. Find out more about the behind-the-scenes of how Cooking PaPa was ideated and built!








As a group of students who wished to promote cooking, we wondered why our peers wouldn’t cook!







We thought that cooking was tough, daunting and tedious for them…








No applications today offer an integrated recipe finding and grocery management system!








In an effort to solve this, Cooking PAPA set off to validate and fill this gap in the market!
